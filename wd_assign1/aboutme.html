My name is Thomas Fitzgerald and I am currently studying at Swinburne Online. I was born and raised in Warrnambool and I am 30 years old. As a part-time online student and full-time guest service agent at Marriott, my life can be quite hectic at times. However, I'm lucky to have the support of my wonderful wife Meg, who is an architect from Japan. Meg and I met in 2019 when I was working abroad in Japan. We quickly hit it off and fell in love. After 2 years of dating, we got married in 2021 and started building our life together. Last November, our lives changed forever when our son Nicholas was born. It's been an incredible journey watching him grow and learn, and Meg has been an amazing mother every step of the way. Despite our busy schedules, Meg and I always make time for each other and for our family. We love exploring Melbourne and trying new restaurants, and we're both passionate about travel and discovering new cultures. As for my studies, I'm currently enrolled at Swinburne Online and studying Tech. It can be challenging balancing work, family, and coursework, but I'm determined to succeed and build a better future for my family. Overall, I feel incredibly grateful for my life and all the amazing people in it. I know that with Meg by my side, anything is possible.
